📌 Important Notes & Policies

  • All fees are payable at the school office or via online transfer (NEFT/UPI).
  • Admission fee is a one-time charge applicable only to new students.
  • Fee must be paid by the 10th of June (Term 1) and 10th of October (Term 2) to avoid late fine.
  • A late fee of ₹50/day will be charged after the due date.
  • Sibling discount of ₹2,000/year is applicable from the second child onwards.
  • Merit scholarship (90%+ students): 50% tuition fee waiver available.
  • Once paid, fees are non-refundable except in extraordinary circumstances at the Principal's discretion.
  • Fee receipts will be issued for every payment — keep them safe for future reference.
