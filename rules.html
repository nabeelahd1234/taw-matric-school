📋 Guidelines

Rules & Regulations

Our rules are designed to create a safe, respectful and joyful environment for all. 🌟

⚠️ Important Notice

All students, parents and staff are expected to read, understand and follow these rules. Adherence to these guidelines ensures a harmonious and productive school environment for everyone. Rules are reviewed annually and updates are communicated through the school diary.

⏰

Attendance & Punctuality

  • 1
    School begins at 8:30 AM. Students must arrive by 8:20 AM.
  • 2
    Minimum 85% attendance is mandatory for exams and promotions.
  • 3
    Late arrivals after 8:35 AM require a written note from parents.
  • 4
    Absence must be notified to the class teacher by 9:00 AM.
  • 5
    Medical leave requires a doctor's certificate upon return.
  • 6
    Planned absences must be requested in advance via school diary.
👔

Uniform & Appearance

  • 1
    Full school uniform is compulsory every day except designated days.
  • 2
    Uniform must be clean, neat and properly ironed at all times.
  • 3
    Black shoes and white socks are part of the standard uniform.
  • 4
    ID card must be worn visibly on the school premises daily.
  • 5
    Hair must be neatly tied or kept; no dyeing or fancy styles.
  • 6
    No jewellery other than small studs or religious threads allowed.
📚

Academic Conduct

  • 1
    Homework must be completed and submitted on time every day.
  • 2
    Textbooks, notebooks and materials must be brought as per schedule.
  • 3
    Cheating or malpractice in any form is strictly prohibited.
  • 4
    Students must actively participate in all classes and activities.
  • 5
    Mobile phones are NOT permitted on school premises.
  • 6
    Library books must be returned within the due date.
🤝

Behaviour & Discipline

  • 1
    Respect for teachers, staff and fellow students is non-negotiable.
  • 2
    Bullying, ragging or any form of harassment is strictly banned.
  • 3
    Students must maintain silence and order in corridors during class hours.
  • 4
    Damage to school property must be reported and compensated.
  • 5
    Littering on campus is not permitted; use dustbins provided.
  • 6
    Use of abusive language is a punishable offence.
🚌

Transport Rules

  • 1
    Board and alight from the bus only at designated stops.
  • 2
    No standing or moving inside the bus while it is in motion.
  • 3
    Respect the bus driver and attendant at all times.
  • 4
    Changes in transport route require prior written approval.
  • 5
    No eating in the school bus to maintain cleanliness.
  • 6
    Students missing the bus must inform parents immediately.
👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Guidelines

  • 1
    Parents must attend all parent-teacher meetings without fail.
  • 2
    Any grievances should be directed to the class teacher first.
  • 3
    Do not visit classrooms during teaching hours without appointment.
  • 4
    Check and sign the school diary every day for communications.
  • 5
    Fees must be paid by the due date; late fees will be charged.
  • 6
    Inform school of any change in emergency contact details promptly.
