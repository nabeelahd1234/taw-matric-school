Admissions 2025-26

Take the first step towards your child's bright future at TAW Matric School! 🌟

📋 Admission Process
1

Fill Application

Complete the online or offline admission form with all required details.

2

Submit Documents

Provide birth certificate, previous school TC and mark sheets.

3

Entrance Test

Attend the simple entrance assessment (classes 1–12) or interaction (LKG/UKG).

4

Parent Meet

Attend a brief counselling session with the Principal and class teacher.

5

Confirm & Pay

Complete fee payment and receive your child's school diary and ID card.

  • ✅ Birth Certificate
  • ✅ Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • ✅ Previous year mark sheet
  • ✅ Aadhar card (child + parent)
  • ✅ 4 passport size photographs
  • ✅ Caste & Community certificate
  • ✅ Medical fitness certificate
