🌟 Our Story

TAW Matric Higher Secondary School was founded in 2005 with a simple but powerful dream — to provide world-class education to every child in our community, regardless of their background.

Over the past two decades, we have grown from a small school with 200 students to a thriving educational institution with over 1,200 students, 85+ qualified teachers, and a sprawling campus equipped with modern facilities.

We are affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Matriculation Board and follow a holistic curriculum that balances academics with sports, arts, and values education. Our graduates go on to excel in top colleges and universities across India and abroad.
