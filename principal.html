✉️ Principal's Message

Dear Parents, Students and Well-Wishers,

It is with immense pride and joy that I welcome you to TAW Matric Higher Secondary School — a place where dreams are nurtured, character is built, and futures are shaped.

At TAW, we believe that every child is a unique gift with boundless potential. Our mission is to create a safe, stimulating and joyful environment where each student can discover their strengths, face challenges with confidence and grow into responsible, compassionate global citizens.

Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure and child-centric curriculum work in harmony to provide a holistic education that goes beyond textbooks. We celebrate every achievement — big or small — and treat every setback as an opportunity to learn and rise stronger.

I invite you to be an active partner in your child's educational journey. Together, let us light the flame of knowledge, curiosity and excellence in every young heart that walks through our doors!

— Dr. A. Rajesh Kumar, Principal 🎓
